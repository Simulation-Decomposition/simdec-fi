SimDec now has its own assistant.

The **SimDec Assistant** is a dedicated GPT designed to help users understand the method, interpret results, and get started with SimDec faster.

[You can try it here](https://chatgpt.com/g/g-69b41f4fa01c819181ed3a7f3256c6b5-simdec-assisstant)

The assistant can help with things like:

- explaining SimDec concepts
- interpreting SimDec charts and scenario structures
- suggesting workflows for simulation analysis
- helping prepare data for SimDec
- answering practical questions about using the method

Instead of searching through papers or documentation, you can simply ask questions and explore ideas interactively.

This is another small step toward making SimDec easier to access and use for anyone working with simulation outputs, uncertainty analysis, or complex models.
