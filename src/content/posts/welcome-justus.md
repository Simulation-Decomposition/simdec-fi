Justus Helo, a Master's student in industrial engineering at LUT University, joins the SimDec team.

Justus will be contributing to 
- improving the [SimDec dashboard](https://simdec.io/) and the [Python package](https://github.com/Simulation-Decomposition/simdec-python/)
- research on efficient variance-based sensitivity indices
- expanding horizons of academic and business use of SimDec 

Many thanks to the sponsors of this position, Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council of Canada (NSERC), Schulich School of Business, York University, and Julian Scott Yeomans.
